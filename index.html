About

The short version

My background brings together engineering, product, and writing. I studied data science at Purdue and wrote for the student newspaper, then moved into product work, writing the PRDs and specs that turn an idea into something a team can build.

At Deloitte I lead a GenAI marketing content platform for Amazon Ads. The engineering and product side is how I build the prompt systems, brand-voice guides, and scoring frameworks that let a model draft copy at scale. The writing side is how I keep the output on brand, with a human editor reviewing it before it ships.

This role needs someone who can build the system that drafts on-brand copy and also judge the copy itself. That mix of engineering, product, and writing is the work I do now.
